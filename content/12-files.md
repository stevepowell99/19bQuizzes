What feature does Causal Map offer for managing versions of your uploaded files?
y Automatic saving every ten minutes and the ability to restore a previous version.
Automatic deletion of all versions older than 30 days to keep storage usage within your account quota.
Conversion of all versions into a single optimised format with a merged change history.
Email notifications sent to all collaborators for every change made to a file.
hint The app saves automatically at regular intervals; you can restore any earlier version.

Why is making a copy of a file often a good idea in Causal Map?
y It ensures you have a personal version that can be edited without affecting the original.
It is a mandatory step before any data analysis can begin in the app.
It reduces file size and speeds up processing by removing redundant version history.
It is required under data protection regulations before sharing files with collaborators.
