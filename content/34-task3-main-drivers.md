A "drivers" framing looks for:

Treating admin-only factors you typed in as drivers, even when they never appear in respondent text.

If two drivers correlate on the pooled map, a cautious analyst:
y Studies whether filters or grouping explain overlap before claiming separate mechanisms
Presenting the two as interchangeable levers — without checking whether synonyms, demographics, or TOC filters stacked the deck.
Dropping the messier one so the slide stays clean, even when quotes still support both under the same filters.
Treating map-side-by-side overlap like a random-effects impact model would — without any extra design justification.
