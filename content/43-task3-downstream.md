Following a factor downstream means tracing:
y Consequences and later links that move away from causes toward effects in the displayed chain logic
Neighbouring nodes that share a colour or band in the automatic layout, treating visual proximity as tantamount to sequential influence even when coded provenance shows unrelated mechanisms
Later sentences inside the same transcript paragraph independent of which links were actually coded from that excerpt, as if reading order inside quotations equals causal order on the map
Every textual mention of the focal label anywhere (including administrative notes or hashtags) even when no directional link was coded from the selected starting factor under active filters

Downstream questioning helps when programmes care about:
y Ripple effects beyond immediate outputs respondents mention
Only the named logframe output nodes, even when respondents still describe additional consequences your current filters have not yet surfaced in summary tables
Antecedent motivations or background conditions respondents happened to mention before programme content, misinterpreted as downstream programme effects because they appear later in transcripts
Whether funders emotionally prefer the visual styling of exported maps, rather than whether credible chains under current filters support claims about longer-run change
