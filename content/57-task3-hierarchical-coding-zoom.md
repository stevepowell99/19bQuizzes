Hierarchical naming (semi-colons) supports Task 3 when you:
y Alternate broad strategic views and narrower operational detail without rewriting everything nightly
Delete detailed child codes whenever you zoom outward — permanently — rather than collapsing them for one summary view backed by quotations.
Explain fine detail-only in prose memos when zoom hides specifics — quotations and bookmarks that carry child codes disappear from teamwork.
Rely on label depth alone — stakeholder questions rarely need filter stacks, exclusions, sentiment views, or custom columns beyond zoom.

If zoom level hides child labels:
y Interpret maps as summaries at chosen granularity — not loss of qualitative evidence
Treat hidden child labels as deleted data — on-screen summaries mean the underlying excerpts must have left the project.
Infer consent was withdrawn because headings roll up for readability — summarising views and consent are separate issues.
Treat rolled-up parent headings as erasing child branches — display simplification is different from losing coded links underneath.
