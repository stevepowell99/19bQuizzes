Viewing causal maps as geometric objects prompts questions about:
y Density of connections, choke points between clusters — before jumping to thematic labels
Battery life on a workshop laptop, mistaken for how solid the thematic clusters are behind dense map regions.
Screen width in the meeting room — not whether layout choices hide or exaggerate bottlenecks you'd see in the quotes.
Your Netlify or SSO tier — irrelevant to how many independent claims sit behind a choke point on the map.

High density around a hub factor can imply:
y Heavy conceptual bundling worth checking naming consistency alongside genuine recurrence
Busy hubs look tidy on labels — you're done naming work — even when quotes show different mechanisms under the same heading.
Prune busy hubs for a cleaner screenshot — even when many people really did point at that junction in their text.
Trust the spoke pattern as enough — skip filters and quotes that show merged or tangled stories piled into one hub.
